Stone Cutters Go Club

Meets Thursday Evenings, 7pm

Runcible Spoon
412 E 6th Street
Bloomington, IN 47408

Contact: Vera Flocke

The Stone Cutters Go Club is located in Bloomington, Indiana. We welcome anyone with an interest in Go to come and visit, new players included! Our goal is to help provide a network for Go players and bring awareness of Go to the community.

Membership is free, but please get a drink or food when you arrive to thank the Runcible Spoon for allowing us to use their space. We supply some boards and stones, but please bring your own if you have a portable set.
